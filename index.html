The Economy
Three Currencies

Every action in Bee's server earns you currency. How you spend it — and how far you're willing to go — is up to you.

Nectar
The everyday currency of the hive. Earned from daily logins, completing tasks, and being an active member of the community.
How to earn: → Daily login bonus → Completing tasks → Gifts from Bee
Amber
An engagement currency earned through sustained dedication. Required to access Bee's most exclusive members-only content.
How to earn: → +1 every daily login → 5-day streak bonus → Special tasks
Aurelia
Ultra-rare prestige currency. Held only by the most devoted of Bee's hive. Cannot be gifted — only forged or granted.
How to obtain: → Forge (50 nectar + 50 amber) → Monthly admin grant → Almost impossible to get
