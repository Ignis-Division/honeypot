The Three Currencies
What you're earning and spending

Honeypot runs on three types of currency. Think of them as different tiers of loyalty — the harder they are to get, the more powerful they are.

Nectar — everyday currency
Amber — members-only access
Aurelia — ultra-rare prestige
Quick summary
Nectar is what you'll use most — you earn it daily and spend it on most shop items. Amber builds up slowly over time and unlocks exclusive members-only content. Aurelia is nearly impossible to get and only spendable on the rarest items in the shop.

You can check your balance of all three at any time using /balance.

Daily & Streaks
Your most consistent source of currency
daily
Free · Once per day

This is the single most important command. Run it every day to claim free nectar and amber. That's it — just show up.

01Type /daily in any bot channel
02You instantly receive nectar and amber deposited to your wallet
03Come back tomorrow — it resets every 24 hours
Streak bonus: If you claim daily for multiple days in a row without missing one, your streak builds up. A 5-day streak unlocks a bonus hex task. Don't break the chain.
VIP perk: If you hold the VIP role, you earn double nectar on every daily claim.
balance
Free

Shows you all three of your wallets — how much nectar, amber, and aurelia you currently have, plus a short list of your most recent transactions so you can see what came in and what went out.

Private: Your balance is only visible to you — it shows as an ephemeral message that no one else can see.

Earning More Currency
Beyond the daily
earn
Free

Pulls up a full list of all available tasks you can complete to earn extra currency. Some tasks are repeatable, some are one-time only, and each has a different cooldown and reward.

01Run /earn to see all available tasks and their rewards
02Find a task you can do — leave a comment, react to a post, recruit someone
03Complete the action in real life, then come back and use /task complete
task complete
Free

After you've actually done a task from the earn list, use this command to claim the reward. You'll pick the task from a dropdown menu and the currency hits your wallet immediately.

Honesty system: Task completion is on your honour. The hive notices who's genuinely showing up and who's not.
example →/task complete · choose "Leave a comment on a post" · receive 2 nectar
How to earn amber
Amber is harder to come by. You get +1 amber every time you claim your daily, plus bonus amber from the 5-day streak task. It builds slowly by design — it's meant to reward fans who stick around.

The Shop
Spending your currency on Bee's exclusive items
shop
Free to browse

Opens Bee's boutique and shows you everything currently available to purchase. Each item shows the price, how many are left in stock, and the item ID you need to buy it.

01Run /shop to browse everything available
02Note the ID number next to the item you want
03Use /buy <id> with that number to purchase it
NSFW items: Some items are hidden by default and only visible to members with the designated members-only role. If you see items listed by others but can't see them yourself, you don't have the role yet.
buy <id>
Costs currency

Purchases a specific item from the shop using its ID number. Before anything is deducted from your wallet, the bot shows you a confirmation screen so you can double-check what you're buying and how much it costs.

01Type /buy 3 (replace 3 with the actual item ID)
02A confirmation embed appears showing the item name, price, and your current balance
03Click ✅ Confirm to proceed, or ❌ Cancel to back out — nothing is charged until you confirm
Auto vs manual items: Some items deliver instantly. Others go into a queue and Bee fulfils them personally — you'll get a DM when it's ready. Your purchase history tracks both.
purchases
Free

Shows your complete history of everything you've ever bought from the shop — what it was, what you paid, and whether it's been fulfilled or is still pending.

Waitlists
For when an item sells out

When an item runs out of stock, you can join its waitlist to be notified the moment it gets restocked. You'll get a DM the second more become available — giving you a head start over anyone who wasn't on the list.

waitlist join <id>
Costs 2 nectar

Joins the waitlist for a sold-out item. It costs 2 nectar to hold your spot — this is fully refunded if you leave the waitlist yourself before the item restocks.

example →/waitlist join 5 · joins waitlist for item #5 · costs 2 nectar
waitlist leave <id>
Refunds your 2 nectar

Removes you from a waitlist and returns your 2 nectar immediately. If you changed your mind or got something else instead, you won't lose your currency.

waitlist view
Free

Shows all the waitlists you're currently on, your position in each queue, and how many other people are ahead of you.

Gifts from Bee
Checking and claiming what's waiting for you

Bee can send gifts directly to your inbox — currency, special rewards, or anything she decides to give you. Gifts don't arrive automatically in your wallet; you have to claim them yourself.

gifts view
Free

Shows everything currently sitting in your gift inbox — what it is, how much, and any message attached. Nothing is added to your balance until you claim it.

gifts claim <id>
Free

Claims a specific gift by its ID and adds it to your wallet. Run /gifts view first to find the ID.

example →/gifts claim 2 · claims gift #2 and adds it to your balance
gifts claimall
Free

Claims every pending gift in your inbox in one go. If you've been away for a while and have multiple gifts waiting, this is the fastest way to collect them all.

Your Rank
Tiers, scores, and the leaderboard

Every nectar you earn adds to your score. As your score grows, you climb through the hive tiers — each new tier gives you a Discord role and a one-time nectar bonus as a reward.

rank
Free

Shows your current tier, your score, and exactly how many more points you need to reach the next tier. Useful for knowing how close you are to an upgrade.

Score is cumulative: Your score counts all nectar you've ever earned — not just what's currently in your wallet. Spending nectar on the shop doesn't lower your rank score.
leaderboard
Free

Shows the top-ranked fans in the server, sorted by score. Your own position is highlighted so you can see where you stand even if you're not in the top 10.

The Forge
Creating aurelia — the rarest currency
forge
Rare · Irreversible

Sacrifices 50 nectar and 50 amber to create 1 aurelia. Aurelia is the most powerful currency in the hive and unlocks Bee's most exclusive shop items — things no one else can access.

01Make sure you have at least 50 nectar and 50 amber (check with /balance)
02Run /forge — a confirmation screen appears before anything is deducted
03Confirm the trade — 50 nectar + 50 amber are permanently consumed and 1 aurelia is added
This cannot be undone. Once forged, the nectar and amber are gone. Aurelia cannot be gifted to other fans — once you have it, it stays with you until you spend it.

Gifting Other Fans
Sending currency to people in the server
gift <user> <amount>
Costs currency

Sends nectar or amber from your wallet to another fan in the server. You pick the person, the amount, and which currency — then confirm before it sends.

01Type /gift and tag the person you want to send to
02Enter the amount and choose nectar or amber
03Confirm — the currency is deducted from you and lands in their wallet
Aurelia cannot be gifted. It can only be created through forging or granted directly by Bee's admins — it's not transferable between fans.
example →/gift @username · amount: 10 · currency: nectar
